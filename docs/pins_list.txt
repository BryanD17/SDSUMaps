Schema contract
---------------
PinID is the value persisted to Firestore as `event.location`
(see docs/DATA_SCHEMA.md, "Collection: events"). The display string in
the "Location" column is UI-only — never persist it. `getLocationById()`
in app/constants/locations.ts is the safe lookup.
